EFG4K Spiral Wire Hose-SAE 100R12

Meets Flame Resistance Acceptance Designation "MSHA 2G" , For Bidegradable Hydraulic Fluids

Application
Extremely high-pressure hydraulic applications. Exceeds all performance requirements for SAF 100R12, EN 856 R12 and EN 856 4SP (-16). Compatible with biodegradable hydraulic fluids like polyolester, polyglycol and vegetable oil as well as standard petroleum-based fluids. Superior impulsw performance: tested to1,000000 impulse cycles.

Construction
Tube: Black, oil resistant, synthetic rubber (Nitrile -Type C).
Reinforcement: Four alternating layers of spiraled, high- tensile steel Wire.
Cover: Black, oil resistant, synthetic rubber (Neoprene -Type A). BLue stripe with print latline.
